Facing difficulties collaborating with fellow classmates?

Students during their studies in the university miss a class like environment. They like to develop skills through interacting with fellow students by making teams, groups and study batches.

During this time all people want things to be online and get things done from home. This platform provides oppurtunity to get all the things together at one place that makes this possible.

Details

Currently the existing websites like Piazza, Stack Overflow etc.
helps students to get clarify their doubts (Though not in a timely manner).
It does not enable the students in continuous study.

Some of the things that lack in these websites are:
  • Interaction with other fellow students.
  • Students can't have any social connectivity with other students.
  • Collectively study and progress in a domain.

Users can:

  • Login using their UMB mail id and join groups.
  • They can post their doubts in discussion forms.
  • Students can find people to team up and make some small time projects.
  • Search for people with similar interests or topics helps in interaction.

My Umass Coop

  • Students and teachers can login using their college mail id.
  • After signing up users can find their dashboard with various options to navigate through website.
  • Students can join/create groups to interact with other students or join their classes and professors will be made admin to class groups.
  • Discussion forms lead to various types of discussion students or teachers post and follow ups can be done.
  • Using Meetings tab professor can conduct a meeting (take class online) and share screen and do a lot of other stuff.
  • Student can get their grades and professors can view cumulative grades of all students in class through groups.
