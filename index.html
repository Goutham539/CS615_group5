Facing difficulties collaborating with fellow classmates?

Students during their studies in the university miss a class like environment. They like to develop skills through interacting with fellow students by making teams, groups and study batches.

Details

Currently the existing websites like Piazza, Stack Overflow etc.
helps students to get clarify their doubts (Though not in a timely manner).
It does not enable the students in continuous study.

Some of the things that lack in these websites are:
  • Interaction with other fellow students.
  • Students can’t find people to team up and make some small time projects.
  • Collectively study and progress in a domain.
